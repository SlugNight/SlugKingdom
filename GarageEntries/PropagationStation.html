Propagation Station

Back to the Garage

A propagation station is something I have wanted to make for a while. Before this I put cuttings into a glass of water and hoped for the best. Although I had good sucess with the ol "bung it in a cup" method I thought it would be nice to give my cuttings a proper place.

The build

It was a very straightforward process with not many hiccups. Unfortunetly I don't have any photos of the process but I do have the final product and my plans:

Initially I wanted wide "feet" with weights but I decided againt this design as joining them withou the correct tools would be a pain. Instead I just gave it a flat base which has worked fine so far, it's not like the plants are moving very much.

Anohter alteration I made was not including the weird plant holder idea. Basically, to make sure the smaller cuttings stayed above the water thin string could be ran across the length of the station. The string would then hold up the cuttings. I also did not include this for two reasons 1. finding appropriate magnets would have been a pain 2. There wasnt acually a need for it as the tubes were so small...
